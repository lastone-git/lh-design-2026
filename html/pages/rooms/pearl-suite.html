Pearl Suite

Our largest and most flexible ground-floor space, with direct courtyard access and a modern finish for events, conferences, training days and celebrations.

130m² / 1399.3 sq. ft 110 standing £75 hourly

The space

Flexible, spacious and ready for larger gatherings.

Our Pearl Suite is situated on the ground floor with doors opening onto the courtyard, which is also available to rent. Renovated in 2024, the room is modern, versatile and well suited to events that need room to move.

  • State of the art AV equipment with two large screens
  • Courtyard access for drinks, breaks and networking
  • Voile curtains for daytime light, privacy or ambience
  • Free high speed wifi
  • Prime North Cardiff location in Llanishen
  • Free on-site and off-road parking
  • Dedicated support staff and visitor toilets
